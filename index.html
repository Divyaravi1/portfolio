Hi! I’m Divya Ravi...

I’m a Product and Experience Designer with a Master of Interaction Design and Electronic Arts from the University of Sydney.

I’m also an accredited architect with a Bachelor’s degree in Architecture from India.

I’ve always been interested in using the design skills I gained through architecture to explore the intersection of technology and human-centered design. To deepen my UX knowledge, I completed seven Google courses as part of the Google UX Design Professional Certificate on Coursera.

This website is my personal exploration of HTML, Bootstrap, and my fascination with creating intuitive and aesthetically pleasing user experiences. My exposure to IT system design and development includes programming in Java, JavaScript, and understanding Object-Oriented Design principles.

In my work as an architect, I’ve contributed to sustainability projects, interior design, graphic design, and parametric architecture—each reinforcing my passion for creative problem-solving and design systems thinking.
