Design is a concept that is generally associated with art and color. While that is primarily true, it is a small portion of what design as a whole encompasses...

A good design fulfills the user's or the subject's needs and requirements. Architecture, product design and, similarly, User interface and User experience are fields in which the design is the primary interface between the subject and its needs. The interaction of the design and the user determines the design's success..

Thesis

This thesis project explores creating a new social space for an exploited workforce, who are primarily women, by reviving an unwanted urban void in the city of Bangalore.

The project is a socially responsible project that revitalizes an unused space in an industrial area to a community space which experiments with the efficiency of urban agriculture and could also be a prototype for other industrial areas in the city.

Process

Addressing a safe social space for working women, especially in the context of overcrowded cities of India.

Project

A project catering to the women workforce and reviving an urban wasteland.
