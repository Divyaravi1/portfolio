Hi!


I'm a Master's student at University of Sydney studying Interaction Design and Electronic Arts!

Design is a concept that is generally associated with art and color. However, it is a multidisciplinary field that can be applied in many areas and focuses on creating something that serves a specific function or solves a specific problem.

A good design fulfils the user's or the subject's needs and requirements. Architecture, product design and, similarly, User interface and User experience are fields in which the design is the primary interface between the subject and its needs. The interaction of the design and the user determines the design's success.
